About

Green, that’s what Blerta means in Albanian language color of hope! My full name is Blerta Bashuri or shortly “BiBi”. Honoured to be part of this course and cohort. I am originally from Albania, a small but strategic country in South-Eastern Europe. Back in my home country, I have studied foreign languages and design. After I was graduated, I started working as a graphic designer for an Italian company. I have always had an interest in exploring the world and learn about diverse cultures. That is why I decided to take a break from my job four years ago and travelled and worked around the world. I joined the world of water cruises. This year, I have started the first steps of my biggest dream-the Canadian Dream.

My education and work experience have allowed me to discover and strengthen some of my strongest skills, such as communication, the spirit of initiative, and creativity. I had learned new skills such as emergency management at the open seas, group leading when I was working as a tour guide around the world and technological skills when I had to use different means of communication in different countries. Working in an environment with hundreds of people from different countries and cultures was unique. I was always trying to create melting pot activities, such as the best competition for the best Halloween pumpkin cravings or the best painting representing our countries and cultures. I consider myself privileged for being a student of this cohort.

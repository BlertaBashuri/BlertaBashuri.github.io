About Artisanal Gelato

Delicious old fashioned ice cream made the way it should be; using fresh 100% Canadian Cream is what we’re all about! 2018 marks the 70th Anniversary of Shaw’s Ice Cream. There is a lot of history behind our brand. Here is our story! Our gelato is made fresh every day in our boutiques, only using natural, select ingredients, including fresh milk, fruit, Piedmont Hazelnut, Green Pistachio from Bronte, Sorrento lemons, Sicilian Almonds, and, of course, our chocolate! Visit us for more flavour in your lives. We welcome everyone from all around.

The rich,flavor of our gelato follows centuries-old traditions, celebrating the genuine, artisan quality of one of Italy's most world-renowned foods, which continues to bring people together. We look forward to serving you our latest creations in our brand new gelato bar “A taste of Italy close to you” Each month, we offer a brand-new recipe so customers can experience different tastes and try inventive, surprising flavors. You can order fresh gelato for pickup or delivery every day from 11am to 10:30pm via UberEats.

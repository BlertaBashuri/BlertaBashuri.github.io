Welcome

Delicious, handcrafted ice cream starts with natural and organic locally-sourced ingredients. It’s a little concept I like to call farm-to-frozen. I personally make all the ice cream which is made in the kitchen just behind the counter, its a small but mighty space! The creative ice creams, including dairy and dairy-free have received accolades both in Ottawa and nationally. Yum Cream is open 7 days a week, even in the winter time.

The shop opened in 2000 and the ice cream that is made here is the best ice cream we know how to make. Quality ingredients is the backbone here and even though prices may be lower then other ice cream shops, its the standard of excellence that we like to be measured by.

Menu

Madagascar Vanilla If you want to know how fresh our ice cream is, give Madagascar Vanilla a try. We use both vanilla beans and extract grown in the Madagascar region. We don't mess around with this robust and full-bodied flavor with notes of bourbon and caramel. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, organic egg yolks, Madagascar vanilla extract and beans.

Chocolate

We use the robust Guittard Rogue chocolate to make this flavor rich and fudgy. It's chocolate ice cream the best way we know how to make it. If you are a chocolate fanatic, we made this one for you. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, Guittard Rogue cocoa powder, organic egg yolks, sea salt, vanilla extract Speckled Milky Chocolate featuring local Terroir Chocolate TC Chocolate in Fergus Falls, Minn is highlighted in this flavor. They are a bean-to-bar chocolatier meaning they purchase raw cocoa beans and do the laborious work of turning these beans into chocolate bars. The canvas of this flavor is a milky chocolate using their 70% cocoa bars. We melt this into Autumnwood Dairy’s milk and cream and then speckle this base, as the ice cream is churning, with their 60% chocolate and you get bits of truffle-like goodness. Seriously local, seriously a must try. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, Terroir Chocolate 60% (organic cocoa beans, organic cane sugar, organic cocoa butter, whole milk powder), Terroir Chocolate 70%(organic cocoa beans, organic cane sugar, organic cocoa butter), organic egg yolks.

Lavender

We steep dried lavender for 2-3 days to create lavender essence. A beautiful purple hue is created by using a bit of beet powder and butterfly pea flower. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, lavender essence, organic egg yolks, organic beet powder, organic butterfly pea flower.

Black Garlic with Chocolate Chips
(contains soy)

This flavor was created during the start of the pandemic and we only sold it by the pint. Now we are scooping it and would highly encourage you to try a sample and this unique umami-rich ice cream. Black garlic is fresh garlic that has been fermented and aged until it turns black. Tasting notes of fig, raisin and balsamic. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, organic black garlic, chocolate chips(cocoa mass, cocoa butter, vegetable oil, soy lecithin), organic egg yolks Local Honeyberry and Raspberry Crisp (contains gluten) This is what ice cream dreams are made of, it’s our best selling ice cream. Super excited about offering you this flavor with honeyberries from Dawson, Minn. Honeyberries are the new super fruit and have been known to be the most nutritious fruit. They are tart and are an amazing blending fruit with raspberries. The crisp is kinda like the topping of apple crisp using Sunrise Flour Mill heirloom whole wheat pastry flour, muscovado sugar, and Hope Creamery butter. Ingredients. Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, local honeyberries, black raspberries, organic oats, Sunrise Flour Mill organic heirloom whole wheat pastry flour, Hope Creamery butter, muscovado sugar, sea salt, baking powder, baking soda, organic egg yolks.

Fresh Rhubarb

Our signature flavor which means it’s a big selling flavor. It’s here through the end of June so take advantage of this seasonal gem. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, fresh organic rhubarb, tapioca, organic egg yolks, Madagascar vanilla extract. Malted Vanilla with All Natural Malted Milk Balls (contains gluten and soy) If you are a malt fan this is your jam! Malt powder combined with Autumnwood Farm dairy is heaven. Strawberry - using local berries!

Strawberries are fresh in June/July here in Minnesota and we take full advantage of that by bringing enough berries during the season and freezing them for year round use. This year we got gorgeous berries from T Berries in Avon, Minn, near my hometown. Most of the berries were picked by my family and I and I’m grateful for our bountiful harvest. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, local T Berries strawberries, organic egg yolks, vanilla extract, fruit acid.

Lemon Infused Olive Oil and Sea Salt

Crazy popular as well as unique. It’s the kiss of lemon in the olive oil that makes this flavor what it is. Don’t expect any tartness, expect creaminess from the olive oil and lemon essence with a kiss of salt. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, lemon infused extra virgin olive oil , sea salt, organic egg yolks Cookies and Cream using Newman O's (contains gluten and soy) We soak Newman O’s in milk and cream in order to get a “cookie milk”. What’s next? Adding even more cookies after this ice cream is churned. We include organic and all-natural Newman O's(no Oreos here) for the cookies. No artificial colors or flavoring. ever. This classic is one of our top sellers. Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, Newman O's (organic wheat flour, organic powdered sugar, organic sugar, organic palm fruit oil, canola oil, cocoa, natural flavors, baking soda, soy lecithin), organic egg yolks, Madagascar vanilla extract.

Ingredients: Autumnwood Farm cream and milk, cane sugar, organic fair trade bananas, organic egg yolks Sea Salt, Caramel, Praline Pecans (contains pecans) We created this flavor 16 years ago and has been a stellar seller since. Non Dairy Muscovado Sugar

Muscovado sugar is a partially unrefined sugar that looks like a very dark brown sugar. The taste is similar to brown sugar however much more of a complex, rich, deeper taste. This dairy free option has tasting notes of butterscotch pudding, toffee, molasses. Ingredients: Organic coconut milk(organic coconut milk, purified water, organic guar gum), muscovado sugar, organic coconut oil, xanthan gum
